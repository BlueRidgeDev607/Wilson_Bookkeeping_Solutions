Bookkeeping Services

Diagnostic Bookkeeping Review

Before beginning any cleanup or ongoing bookkeeping work, we need to understand exactly where your books stand today. A diagnostic review identifies specific issues in your financial records and creates a roadmap for accurate and up-to-date books.

Includes:

  • Complete review of QuickBooks Online file
  • Review of bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Identification of uncategorized, duplicate, or incorrect transactions
  • Assessment of your balance sheet, profit & loss statement, and critical accounts
  • Written summary of findings with recommendations
  • Detailed scope and pricing for recommended corrective services

Services & Pricing FAQs

I provide three main services: Monthly Bookkeeping & Financial Reporting (ongoing service with P&L and Balance Sheet included), Catch-Up Services (completing missed monthly bookkeeping), and Cleanup Services (fixing messy or incorrect books).

Yes, I'm a QuickBooks Certified ProAdvisor and specialize in QuickBooks Online. I require all clients to maintain an active QuickBooks Online subscription.

Monthly bookkeeping starts at $150/month for up to 100 transactions, with additional fees for higher transaction volumes. Most small businesses fall within the base price range.

Catch-up bookkeeping is priced at the regular monthly rate for each month that needs to be completed. For example, if you're 3 months behind, you'd pay for 3 months of service.

Cleanup projects receive custom flat-fee quotes based on the complexity and scope of work needed. I'll review your situation and provide a fixed price with no surprises.

Catch-Up is for businesses that simply haven't done their regular bookkeeping for several months. Cleanup is for books that have errors, duplicates, unreconciled accounts, or other issues that need to be sorted out and corrected.
